Evangelism

Wherever there's a need — a village, an orphanage, a hospital ward — Hope Now's team is there to help, and to share the reason for the hope they have.

Village missions Ray of Hope Christmas Mission

Many people find their way to the Hope Now office in Cherkassy, and the team there shows God's love to everyone who comes. But much of the work happens out on the road.

To the villages

Hope Now supports Anastasiia and her team of young missioners. Through the year they organise activities and Bible teaching for children living in remote villages, where there is often little else for them.

To the internat

Girls from our pre-independence home visit the internat to share hope and love with the children living there — passing on the care they have received themselves.

To the hospital

Valerii Petrochenko visits the children's hospital regularly, taking gifts and sharing the Gospel with young patients and their families.

To the visually impaired

Life is hard in Ukraine for people with disabilities. The “Ray of Hope” group encourages the visually impaired, supported by Pastor Peter.
