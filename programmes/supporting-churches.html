Supporting Churches

Supporting Churches

Hope Now comes alongside small Ukrainian churches — supporting their pastors, helping with buildings and heating, and training the next generation of leaders.

Bible School Pastors Buildings & heating

Across the Cherkassy region, small churches do faithful work with very little. Hope Now helps them keep going, and helps raise up the pastors they need.

Coming alongside the churches

Hope Now actively helps a number of churches — supporting pastors, helping smaller congregations buy or adapt a building to meet in, providing building materials or heaters, and arranging twinning with UK churches for mutual encouragement.

We visit regularly to encourage them and to find out what they need.

Cherkassy Centre for Biblical Studies

Founded in 2004 under the leadership of a British Baptist minister, the Cherkassy Centre for Biblical Studies trains future pastors and missionaries. It is non-denominational and evangelical, and in May 2006 its first twenty-five students graduated.

Many more have followed since. Churches that once had untrained pastors now have trained ones, and churches that had no pastor at all now do. The work has grown, and the Baptist Bishop of Cherkassy, who has visited the school, is right behind it.
